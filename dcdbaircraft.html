Seat Rental Program

Your Aircraft Stays
Airworthy While Seats Are Refurbished

Aircraft weight and balance requirements mean removing seats from your cabin can ground your plane entirely. DCDB Aircraft Solutions solves this with our exclusive seat rental program: we place certified, airworthy replacement seats directly into your cabin during the refurbishment period. Your aircraft maintains its required weight compliance and configuration — so your flight schedule continues without interruption while your seats are being transformed.

Installed in Your Cabin
Certified replacement seats are fitted directly into your aircraft — not a swap to a different plane — keeping your ship flying on your schedule.
Weight & Balance Maintained
Rental seats are matched to your aircraft's weight and CG requirements so airworthiness and FAA compliance are never compromised.
Zero Downtime to Your Fleet
Because your aircraft remains airworthy throughout, there is no gap in operations — your missions, charter obligations, or personal travel continue unaffected.
Seamlessly Managed by Our Team
We coordinate the logistics of seat rental, installation, and removal around your refurbishment timeline — one less thing for you to manage.

Backlit Flooring
& Countertops

DCDB Aircraft Solutions is the only operation in the industry authorized to perform this refurbishment under full FAA compliance. We hold the Supplemental Type Certificate (STC) — making this an exclusively DCDB service.

Imagine stepping into a cabin where the floor itself glows beneath your feet and countertop surfaces radiate a luminous warmth — all engineered to aircraft standards. Our proprietary backlit flooring and countertop system transforms VIP interiors into a truly one-of-a-kind experience, blending architectural lighting design with aviation-grade materials and full FAA certification.

STC-Certified Installation — We hold the Supplemental Type Certificate, meaning every installation is legally approved and airworthy under FAA regulations.
Aviation-Grade Materials — Lighting systems, substrates, and finishes are all specified and tested to meet aircraft flammability, weight, and vibration standards.
Fully Bespoke Design — Color temperature, panel layouts, translucent surface materials, and light intensity are customized to your cabin's unique aesthetic.
Floors & Countertops — Both surfaces available, individually or as a complete integrated cabin lighting system.
Exclusively DCDB — No other operator holds this STC. If you want backlit flooring or countertops in your aircraft, this is the only FAA-compliant path.
